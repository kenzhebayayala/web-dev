Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Pancetta or bacon 100g
Eggs 2
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Black pepper 1 tsp
  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine hot pasta with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Stir quickly and add pepper.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 12 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 1/2 cup
Egg 1
Flour 1 cup
Chocolate chips 1/2 cup
  1. Mix butter and sugar together.
  2. Add egg and mix well.
  3. Add flour and stir until dough forms.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls on a baking tray and bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes.
